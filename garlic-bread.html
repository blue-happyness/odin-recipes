Parmesan Garlic Bread Recipe

Hello! Today I will be showing you how to make Parmesan Garlic Bread. It's very easy to make, and who doesn't love garlic bread?

Ingredients

  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • ¼ teaspoon dried rosemary
  • ⅛ teaspoon dried basil
  • ⅛ teaspoon dried thyme
  • ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, halved lengthwise

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together melted butter, Parmesan cheese, garlic salt, rosemary, basil, thyme, and garlic powder. Spread half of the mixture onto each piece of French bread. Sprinkle with additional Parmesan cheese if desired. Transfer bread, seasoned-side up, to a medium baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the edges are very lightly browned.
